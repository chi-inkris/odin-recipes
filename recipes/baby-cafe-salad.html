Baby Cafe Salad

A vegan salad with an Asian flair

Ingredients

  • green lettuce of your choice (romaine/spinach/butter/kale)
  • salad topper nuts (sunflower seeds, pumpkin seeds, pine nuts)
  • juliiened carrots
  • thin slices of red onions
  • dried cranberries

dressing

  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 0.5 - 1 tablespoon soy sauce, depending on taste
  • 1 teaspoon apple cider or rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoons strong dijon mustard
  • pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Arrange vegetables on plates as desired.
  2. Whisk all dressing ingredients together in a bowl
  3. Spoon dressing over plated salad.
